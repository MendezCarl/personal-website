### 05/09/2026
I finally got around to create a personal website. I knew I wanted to make a minimalistic website. 

### inspo

I took inspiration from [Bin Wang](https://www.binwang.me/about.html) and [Tai Sanh Nguyen](https://taisnguyen.github.io/#/home). I really enjoyed their simple easy on the eye styling and wanted something simple that gets to the point. Recently I have been interested on documenting events in my life so I decided to also have a blog/entry page like what Bin has on his personal site.

### Tech Stack

- Vite 8 (build tool)
- React + TypeScript
- marked

### Entry system
For the entry system I wanted a system where I just upload markdown files which would have my notes/ideas/writing and display them on the site. I did this with the marked library.
