### 04/24/2026 - 04/30/2026

I went to LAHacks 2026 with my hackathon buddy Anthony Le. Both of us have only seen LA through movies or social media so we both were really exicted to go to the iconic city. It felt so surreal when we arrived in LA and started driving around seeing parts of the city we seen in games or movies.

### food

The main thing I was excited for was the food. We always heard about how good In-N-Out was through media making it a must on this trip. It was definely one of the top burgers I had. Definely would recommend others to atleast try it once. I regret not getting In-N-Out again.

Due to the limited time we didn't get much time to explore the city due locking in on the hackathon. We mainly ate food provided by the venue. The food was alright for a hackathon.

We got the opportunity to eat a our teammates Sina's housing dining hall. This was suprising because he lived off campus with a place not associated with UCLA. It was suprising to see that they had a whole dining hall which offered a variety of foods. It was pretty good and I would definely eat there again.

### hackathon

Based on all the hackathons I been too this one was organized pretty well. I really enjoyed how they gave use virtual tickets which could be added to our digial wallets. This made it easy to not loose a QR code or ID needed to be at the venue.

The tracks and challenges offered were amazing too. I believe the LAHacks team did a great job securing sponsors that provided relevant challenges to the hackers further contributing to a amazing environment for hacking. The venue was great too. It was something unique to be in a arena in the middle of the court.
